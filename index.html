Ladddduuuu Page 01

TO Dear Ladddu,

Har roz jab main WhatsApp kholta hoon aur tumhara naam screen par dikhta hai, mujhe lagta hai meri duniya start ho gayi. Jaise phone unlock karte hi sab kuch normal se special ho jaata hai. Tumhare “hmmm”, “achaaa”, “pagal ho kya?”, “jaan…” ye sirf words nahi hain. Ye mere din ka mood set karte hain. Agar tum reply late karo na, toh pura din ajeeb lagta hai. Aur agar tum ek simple sa “khana kha liya?” bhej do, toh lagta hai duniya me sab theek hai.

Hamari story kisi perfect love story jaisi seedhi nahi thi. Shuruat hui ek random Instagram group se — Chai Sutta Bar. Phir beech me wo fake admin wala confusion, meri stupid reaction, tumhara hurt hona, phir meri realization aur apology. Shayad wahi se sach me hamari kahani start hui. Kyunki agar tum us din mujhe ignore kar deti na, toh shayad aaj main itna lucky nahi hota.

Phir dheere dheere chats badhni shuru hui. Pehle normal talks. Phir thoda personal. Phir Snapchat streaks. Phir late night calls. Phir good morning aur good night ke messages compulsory ho gaye. Mujhe yaad hai wo din jab tumne bola tha, “tum alag ho.” Us din se maine khud ko alag feel karna shuru kiya — sirf tumhare liye.

Tumhari ek baat mujhe sabse zyada pasand hai — tum mujhe daantti bhi ho aur care bhi karti ho. Jab main overthink karta hoon, tum simple sa bol deti ho, “itna mat socha karo, main hoon na.” Ye chaar words mere liye therapy hain. Jab main irritate hota hoon, tum patience se handle karti ho. Jab main galti karta hoon, tum mujhe samjhati ho. Har baar maaf karna easy nahi hota, par tumne kiya. Aur ye main kabhi bhool nahi sakta.

19 September ka din meri life ka sabse special din rahega. Tum pink-peach kurti me, light sky-blue jeans me, white sneakers ke saath, open hair aur haath me bouquet liye hue. Main bas tumhe dekh raha tha. Jab tum mere paas aayi aur humne pehla hug kiya, mujhe laga ki jitni bhi dooriyan thi sab khatam ho gayi. Wo first hug, wo first kiss, wo forehead kiss — sab kuch itna pure tha ki main words me explain nahi kar sakta.

Resultant me baithna. Bombay Taste me hasi. Civil Line ki walk. Subhedhar Ganj Station ke paas ki baatein. Sangam par khade hokar future ki baat karna. Har jagah tum thi, aur tumhare saath main complete tha. Priyanshi, main perfect nahi hoon. Kabhi kabhi ziddi ho jaata hoon. Kabhi overthink karta hoon. Kabhi unnecessary react kar deta hoon. Par ek cheez pakki hai — main tumhe kabhi intentionally hurt nahi chahta.

Hamne decide kiya hai na — kabhi alag nahi honge. Duniya se lad lenge, par ek dusre ka haath nahi chhodenge. Efforts end tak karenge. Aur end tab nahi aayega jab tak hum officially saath nahi hote. Tum meri life ka sabse real part ho. Koi filter nahi. Koi acting nahi. Sirf sachcha pyaar.

Aur haan… agar kabhi future me tum ye padho, toh yaad rakhna — ek ladka hai jo tumhe har roz choose karta hai. Har fight ke baad bhi. Har misunderstanding ke baad bhi. Har situation me.

Tum ho toh sab hai. ❤️

LOVEEEEE YOUUUUU

Laddddduuuuu,

19 September ka wo pehla hug yaad hai? Civil Line ki hawa, tumhari halki si nervous smile, aur jab tum mere paas aayi… us moment me time ruk gaya tha. Par us din se pehle bhi hazaaron virtual hugs the.

Jab tum hostel me ro deti thi aur main sirf message bhej pata tha — “idhar aa jao, tight hug.” Jab tumhari tabiyat kharab hoti thi aur main phone ke through hi keh pata tha — “apna khayal rakho, meri jaan ho.”

Virtual hug ka matlab sirf emoji nahi hota. Wo trust hota hai. Wo reassurance hota hai. Wo yeh hota hai ki chahe duniya kitni bhi against ho jaye, ek insaan hai jo tumhe kabhi chhodkar nahi jayega.

Chahe future me kitne bhi problems aayein, hum kabhi alag nahi honge. Duniya se ladenge, par ek dusre se nahi. Aur jab tak officially saath nahi hote, tab tak har raat ek virtual hug mandatory hai. ❤️
